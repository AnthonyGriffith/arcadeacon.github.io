Cactus McCoy 2

This is the sequel to Cactus McCoy that you have been waiting for! A chance meeting with Ella Windstorm, the last of the winged Volados, sends you on an epic treasure hunting journey. Can you survive an onslaught of new enemigos, wild bosses, and precision platforming on your way to the mythical Ruins of Calavera? Use new weapons and new stats to find Ella, and keep the Serpent Blade from the hands of Malana Mire.
